3.) Maldek:

Maldek was a planet orbiting our sun between the orbits of Mars and Jupiter. A very long time ago, a nuclear war erupted on the planet. One big nuclear weapon penetrated into an underground nuclear weapon storage facility. One big explosion followed by a huge explosion blew up the planet into pieces. The asteroid belt between the orbits of Mars and Jupiter is the result of the destruction of the planet Maldek. 

                                           Juntong Liu,  2019
